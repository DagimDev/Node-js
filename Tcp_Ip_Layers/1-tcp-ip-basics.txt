TCP/IP FUNDAMENTALS
-------------------
1. TCP (Transmission Control Protocol):
   - Ensures reliable data delivery over networks.
   - Breaks data into packets, reassembles at destination.

2. IP Addresses:
   - Unique identifier for devices (e.g., 192.168.1.67).
   - Router shares one IP for multiple devices.

3. Port Numbers:
   - Identifies specific apps/services (e.g., :80 for HTTP).
